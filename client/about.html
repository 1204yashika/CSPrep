About Us

CSPrep is a website that has been designed for students who are preparing for exams like GATE and UGCNET but do not have enough time to prepare. The website offers the opportunity to take exams related to one topic or all topics. The website has been created with the aim of providing an easy and convenient way for students to prepare for their exams. It allows them to take exams at their own pace and in their own time, without having to worry about attending classes or following a strict schedule.
